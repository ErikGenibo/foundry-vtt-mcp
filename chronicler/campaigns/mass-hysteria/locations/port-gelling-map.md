# Port Gelling - Location Guide

> The main settlement on Antares Prime, built around the Drift Beacon. Less than 5 years old.

---

## Overview

Port Gelling is a frontier boomtown that sprang up when the Drift Beacon activated and opened the Antares system to travel. It's part spaceport, part market, part wild west town - all under the watchful eye of Nana Sweetie and her Sweetiebots.

**Population:** Several thousand (fluid - spacers come and go)
**Government:** Nana Sweetie (unofficial but absolute)
**Law Enforcement:** Sweetiebot Patrols + Fr3d

## Key Locations

### Spaceport
- **Docking Bays:** Standard berths for freighters and smaller vessels
- **Hangars:** Long-term storage and major repairs
- **Fuel Depot:** Refined fuel, decent prices
- **Honest Adrogall's Ship Mall:** Ship sales, legitimate quality

### Market District
The commercial heart of Port Gelling. Chaotic, crowded, never sleeps.

| Shop | Owner | Specialty |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Kill | Katar (Vesk) | Weapons |
| The Cocoon | The Shield (Barathu) | Armor |
| UPGRADE | Cog (Cyborg) | Cybernetics, augmentations |
| Asimov's Drones | Asimov (Human) | Robotics, drones |
| Oddz'n'Endz | Oddz & Endz (Skittermanders) | General goods, salvage |
| One Stop Pawnshop | Krull/Shuuthul/Jim | Trade, appraisal, everything |
| Clinic | Doctor Bobo (Skittermander) | Medical care |
| Happy Varmint | "Dr" Fowl (Sarcesian) | Pharmaceuticals |
| Chk Chk's Storage | Chk Chk (Shirren) | Secure storage, molting rooms |
| Herman's Stand | Herman (Human) | Squoxes (unsuccessful) |

### Hospitality Row
- **Gelling Bar and Grill:** Todd's place. Drinks are excellent. Sympathy is not on the menu.
- **All Day Cafe:** Quiet tea shop. Respite from chaos.

### Nana's Compound
- **Sweetiebot HQ:** Where the bots are maintained and deployed from
- **Guest Quarters:** "Comfortable" rooms for people Nana wants close
- **Nana's Office:** Tea, cookies, and ruthless calculation
- **M.A.D.S. Station:** Elite Sweetiebot guards

## Hidden Areas

### The Tunnels (Maintenance Level)
- Goblins used to squat here (before Guk's crew was eliminated)
- Access to Port Gelling's infrastructure
- Possible route to avoid Sweetiebot surveillance

### Water Reclamation Station
- Industrial area, less traffic
- Where Guk would have met with Ringo (if he'd lived)

### The Foundations
> "When Nana was building Port Gelling, the construction crews hit something. Old. Sealed. She had them build around it."

- Precursor structure beneath the port
- Nana knows it's there
- Deliberately built around, not through
- **Future plot point**

## Atmosphere Notes

Port Gelling feels like:
- A frontier boomtown with neon signs
- Wild West meets Blade Runner
- Everyone's running from something or chasing something
- Cheerful Sweetiebots everywhere, always watching
- Nana's presence felt even when she's not visible
